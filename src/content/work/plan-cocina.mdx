<section class="workPost__section workCase">
  <article class="workCase__item">
    <h3 class="workCase__title">Insight</h3>
    <ul class="workCase__list">
     <li class="paragraph">People with busy routines need to prepare quick and easy recipes because their days are very hectic.</li>
     <li class="paragraph">People take a long time to decide to cook and the process involved (deciding, preparing ingredients, cutting if necessary, cooking, serving).</li>
    </ul>
  </article>
  <article class="workCase__item">
    <h3 class="workCase__title">Solution</h3>
    <ul class="workCase__list">
     <li class="paragraph">An app that offers a diverse catalog of recipes with preparation times of no more than 1 hour, filterable by time of day and diet type.</li>
     <li class="paragraph">An app that includes a calendar where they can organize the recipes they prepare during the week, optimizing decision-making time in their day-to-day.</li>
    </ul>
  </article>
</section>
